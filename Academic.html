BA

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) is an undergraduate degree program that typically takes three to four years to complete. It covers a wide range of disciplines such as humanities, social sciences, and languages.

BBS

The Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on business management and administration. It aims to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed for a career in business.

B Ed

The Bachelor of Education (B Ed) is an undergraduate degree program that prepares students for a career in teaching. It includes courses in education theory, teaching methods, and practical teaching experience.
