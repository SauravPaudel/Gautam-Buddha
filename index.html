Course We Offer

Gautam Buddha Multiple Campus Offer multiple courses to enhance students knowledge

BA

Tribhuvan University (TU) in Nepal offers a comprehensive Bachelor of Arts (BA) program under the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences (FOHSS). This program is designed to provide students with theoretical and practical knowledge across a wide range of subjects, making it one of the largest faculties at TU.

BBS

The Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) program at Tribhuvan University (TU) is a comprehensive four-year degree designed to develop skilled managers and entrepreneurs. The program is structured to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in business management, preparing students for various roles in the business sector.

B Ed

The Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) program at Tribhuvan University aims to prepare qualified educators with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in teaching and educational administration. The program is offered in various specializations to cater to the diverse needs of the education sector.

Our Campus

Our facilities

Lab

Our college boasts state-of-the-art laboratory facilities designed to provide students with hands-on learning experiences in various scientific and technical fields. Each lab is equipped with modern instruments and technology, enabling students to conduct experiments, perform research, and apply theoretical knowledge in a practical setting. The labs are maintained by experienced technicians and faculty members who ensure a safe and conducive environment for fostering innovation and critical thinking among students.

Playground

The college's expansive playground is a hub for sports and recreational activities, promoting physical fitness and well-being among students. With dedicated areas for different sports such as soccer, basketball, cricket, and track and field events, the playground offers ample space for both organized sports and casual play. It is a place where students can unwind, build teamwork and leadership skills, and develop a lifelong appreciation for physical activity.Regular sports events and tournaments are organized to encourage participation and foster a spirit.

Library

Our comprehensive library is a cornerstone of the college, offering an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support the academic and personal growth of students. The library provides a quiet and comfortable environment for study and research, equipped with reading rooms, computer stations, and access to online databases. Knowledgeable librarians are available to assist students in finding the resources they need and developing effective research skills.
